Our Story

Founded in 2025, LanJing Ship Service emerged as a dedicated partner for the maritime industry, with a focus on the Indonesian market. Our name "LanJing" reflects our commitment to blue ocean (Lan) and precision (Jing) in service.

Based in Anqing City, Anhui Province -- a historical hub of shipbuilding in China -- we combine local expertise with global standards to deliver comprehensive marine equipment supply and technical solutions.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between advanced marine technology and practical operational needs, ensuring our clients receive reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions.
